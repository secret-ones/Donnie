Poem

You, Always With You'

' Love was never meant to exist only in easy days. It was meant for the moments when dreams feel distant, when doubts grow louder than hope, and when the weight of the world seems too much to carry. I know there will be days when the road becomes uncertain, when plans change, and fears begin to settle in. There will be victories worth celebrating, and failures that leave quiet scars behind. But none of those things could ever change the place you hold in my heart. I want to be there for every chapter, not just the beautiful ones. For the days filled with laughter and excitement, and for the nights when everything feels overwhelming. For the dreams that come true, and for the dreams that need time to heal. Because love is not measured by perfect moments. It is found in staying, in believing, in choosing the same person again and again through every season life brings. No matter how much the world changes, no matter how many storms come and go, my heart will continue to root for yours. Not because life will always be kind, or because every road will be smooth, but because loving you has never depended on circumstances or outcomes. And years from now, when time has carried us through countless days, I hope the love we built will tell the story of two hearts that never stopped believing in one another, through every sunrise and sunset, through every struggle, through everything.




'The Life We Haven't Lived Yet'

Sometimes I catch myself smiling at memories we haven't made yet. I think about the mornings we'll wake up together, the little arguments over what to eat, the random dances in the kitchen, and the nights we'll spend talking about everything and nothing until sleep quietly steals our words. I wonder what our home will look like, what pictures we'll hang on the walls, what dreams we'll chase hand in hand, and how many times we'll laugh so hard that we forget why we started laughing in the first place. I know life won't always be easy. There will be days that test us, moments that ask more from us than we think we can give. But I don't fear those days. Because when I imagine my future, I don't imagine perfection. I imagine you. I imagine choosing you on ordinary days, loving you when we're tired, holding on when life becomes difficult, and growing old with a heart that still skips a beat every time you smile at me. And years from now, when our hair begins to silver and time has written its gentle lines upon our faces, I hope I'll still look at you with the same wonder I carry today. And I'll whisper to you "Out of all the futures I could have dreamed of, I'm grateful that I get to live this one with you."




'A Mile Away From You'

No matter how far the world stretches between us, my heart still finds its way to you. Through late-night calls and quiet messages, through days we miss each other more than words can say, I carry your love with me. There are moments when I wish I could hold your hand, see your smile without a screen between us, and hear your laughter beside me instead of through my phone. But even with the distance, you make my days brighter and my nights gentler. Thank you for loving me from afar, for staying when things get hard, for trusting us when patience is all we have. You remind me that love is not measured by miles, but by the little things we choose every day. I promise to keep choosing you, through every sunrise we do not share, through every countdown until we finally meet again. Because you are not just someone I love, you are my peace, my favorite hello, and the home my heart keeps running back to. So until the day I can hold you close, know that every "good morning," every "I miss you," and every whispered prayer carries your name. And no matter how many miles separate us, my love for you will always find its way home to you.




'Ako Muna, Mahal Ko.'

Hindi mo kailangang magpaliwanag. Hindi mo kailangang ayusin ang sarili mo bago ka humarap sa akin. Huwag mong pilitin ang ngiting hindi naman abot sa iyong mga mata, Dahil dito, sa tabi ko, ayos lang na maging wasak. Ayos lang na maging pagod. Kapag ang mundo ay masyado nang mabigat, Ibigay mo sa akin ang kalahati ng pasanin. Hindi ako nandito para sabihing "kaya mo 'yan," Nandito ako para sabihing, "kung hindi mo na kaya, ako muna." Ako muna ang tatayo para sa ating dalawa. Ako muna ang magsisilbing liwanag sa iyong madilim na eskinita. Hindi ako matatakot sa iyong mga luha, Dahil alam ko ang halaga ng bawat patak nito. Kahit itaboy mo pa ako, kahit sabihin mong "iwan mo na ako," Mananatili ako—hindi bilang istorbo, kundi bilang iyong anino. Dahil ang tunay na pagsama ay hindi lang sa tagumpay. Nandito ako sa pinakamababang bahagi ng iyong buhay, Sa mga gabing wala kang masabi kundi buntong-hininga, At sa mga umagang ayaw mo nang bumangon sa kama. Hindi kita bibitawan. Hindi dahil kailangan kita, Kundi dahil alam kong sa ilalim ng lahat ng bigat na ito, Nandoon pa rin ang taong hinding-hindi ko isusuko. Huminga ka lang. Nandito ako. At hinding-hindi ako aalis.
